# Privacy Policy (GDPR)

This Privacy Policy explains how De’ciZhen collects, uses, and protects personal data when you use the platform.

- Security/session data (auth tokens, login events)
- Marketplace activity (requests, offers, contracts, messages)

## Why We Process Data
- To provide the service and secure user accounts
- To prevent abuse and fraud
- To meet legal obligations

## Your Rights
You may request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of your personal data according to applicable law.
